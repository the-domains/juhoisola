# Events

**Nordic Post Trade 2016 (16.2-17.2.2016).** Nordic Post Trade 2016 is an annual seminar around back-office processes in financial services. The 2016 event focuses on digital transformation, regulatory changes, and digital disruption. Me and my company TAVIQ will give a speech on FinTech startups and new digital business models. [http://talentumevents.fi/nordic-post-trade-helsinki/][0]

**Regional conference by Council of Oulu Region (2.11-3.11.2015). **The second day of this annual conference focused on digital transformation and the opportunities and challenges that it brings to peripheral regions. I gave a speech that emphasized the challenges caused by fragmented European digital market as well as the need to embrace the understanding and skills of the millennial generation.
